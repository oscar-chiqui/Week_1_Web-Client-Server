A Brief Summary

The city of Cuenca is a city with a long history due to the fact that the Spanish colonization influenced it in a cultural and territorial way. The name Cuenca comes from a Spanish city also called Cuenca.
The architecture of Cuenca is like seeing Europe up close due to its remarkable architectural resemblance. the houses, the churches, and the street system are very similar to many Spanish and Italian cities.

My favorite places to visit in Cuenca

  1. See the Casas Colgadas (Hanging Houses)
  2. Visit The Museum ( Arte Abstracto )
  3. The National park " El Cajas "
  4. The local markets ( farmers ) local food.
